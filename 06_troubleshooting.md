---
title: Troubleshooting Guide
---

Problem: Compression Artifacts
Symptoms: Sections thinner than programmed, wrinkled appearance 
Causes: Dull knife, excessive feed rate, wrong clearance angle 
Solutions:
Reduce cutting speed 
Increase clearance angle
Install sharper knife
Reduce feed rate
Monitor cutting force
Problem: Chatter Patterns
Symptoms: Regular oscillation marks on sections 
Causes: Vibration, loose mounting, resonance 
Solutions:
Frequency analysis of vibration source
Improve isolation
Optimize cutting parameters
Check block mounting security
Verify microtome calibration
Problem: Edge Chipping
Symptoms: Irregular edge, debris in sections 
Causes: Knife dullness, excessive force, brittle fracture 
Solutions:
Low feed rates (0.1-0.2μm)
Approach angle optimization
Knife sharpness verification
